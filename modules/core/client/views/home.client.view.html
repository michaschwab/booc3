Meet booc.io
 
A system for linear and non-linear exploration of educational concepts
How does it work?
  • 1. Organizes your course by the learning concepts within and the connections between these concepts.
  • 2. Helps define personalized learning plans through course material to achieve your goal.
  • 3. Integrates material from many sources to give you both the big picture and the detail.
Features
  •  
    Overview of the course
  •  
    Faster path to learn something specific
  •  
    Additional materials linked to each concept
